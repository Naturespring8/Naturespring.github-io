How the solution works

The solution would work in the first place as an individual.

It is sort of encouraging individuals to work on their trash, but we will ask you as an individual to start practicing phytoremediation or simply just planting to improve the soil quality through affected soil or land. Ofcourse we don't want to be alone while improving our soil, many countries are doing this practice already like North America. The solution works as the plant grows, the root of the plant will release nutrients that will be needed for microorganisms and the microbes will reduce the polluted area itself.
