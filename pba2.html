Solution Summary

By practicing our soultion which is Phytoremediation it will benefit a lot in our environment, especially the quality of the soil. What is Phytoremediation? Phytoremediation is the use of plants to degrade or remove the contaminated or polluted areas from soil and also ground water. Simply the practice of phytoremediation is a bioremediation that is traditionally used to make an improvement for our environment and many people actually benefit from it especially the soil. Bioremediation is a use of microbes known for breaking down and cleaning up toxins.
